Target Audience

The target audience for this website is people in the Idaho area wanting to know what the current weather is in the area. We want to ensure that people are able to find the information they need quickly because it should not take long to find information such as the weather.

Persona #1

Mary is a college student stugying at BYU-Idaho. She loves planning activites with her friends and enjoys going to Targhee, Yellowstone, and other outdoor places when she can.

Persona #2

John is a recently married person living in the eastern Idaho area. He has two children, and works as a construction worker. He loves to ski and do things outside in his free time.

Scenario #1

Mary wants to look up the weather for a day at the St Anthony Sand Dunes. She wants to know how many layers she should tell everyone to bring.

Scenario #2

Mary wants to find information on the weather to know if it will rain on Monday for FHE. If it rains she will tell her FHE group that they should consider doing sommething inside. If it will not rain, she will likely ask the group to do something outside if they can.
